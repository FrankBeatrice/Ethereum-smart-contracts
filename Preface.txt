- What is Ethereum?

Ethereum is a software that ensures that data and small computer programs
called "Smart Contracts" are copied and processed on all computers on a given network
without a single cooridinator.

Vision
Ethereum's vision was to create a world of self-sustaining, censorship-resistant
decentralised world computer.

"A computer that can never be altered or controlled by a single power"

Purpose
Ethereum is programmable, so you can also use it for various different digital assets 

Ethereum is a blockchain techology
Chances are you have heard of bitcoin, the main difference between the two, is that bitcoin is strictly used as a transactional currency.
Ethereum can be made to transfer funds, but to also trigger an action.
